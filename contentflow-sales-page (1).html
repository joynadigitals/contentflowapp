The Process

Four steps from offer
to content that converts

ContentFlow is offer-first by design. It starts with what you sell — then builds everything around it.

1
Build your Brand Profile
Your niche, audience, tone of voice, credentials, and the words you never want to use again. The AI reads this for every generation.
2
Add your offers
Every product, service, or programme you sell — with pain points, transformation, objections, and CTA. This is the brief the AI works from.
3
Choose your format and goal
Post, reel, carousel, email, 7-day or 30-day plan. Leads, sales, engagement, or authority. The AI calibrates every word to your goal.
4
Publish content that sells
Copy, schedule, or add to your calendar. Your content is ready — psychology-led, platform-native, and built to be pushed by the algorithm.
